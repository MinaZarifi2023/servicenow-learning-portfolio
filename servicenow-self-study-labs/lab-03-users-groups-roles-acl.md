# Lab 03 – Users, Groups, Roles, and ACL Fundamentals

## Objective

Understand how access and responsibility are structured in ServiceNow.

## Concepts

- User
- Group
- Role
- Assignment Group
- ACL
- Role-based access control

## Example

A service desk agent may have access to view and update incidents, but a normal employee may only be able to submit a request or view their own tickets.

## Steps

1. Study the relationship between users, groups, and roles.
2. Document examples of common roles.
3. Understand why assignment groups are important.
4. Review what ACLs are used for.
5. Create a small diagram or notes showing:
   - User belongs to Group
   - Group has Role
   - Role gives access to table or field

## Reflection

Users, groups, roles, and ACLs are essential for secure enterprise workflows. They make sure that the right people have the right level of access.
