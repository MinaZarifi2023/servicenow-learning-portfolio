# Lab 02 – Incident Management Basics

## Objective

Understand the lifecycle and key fields of an incident in ServiceNow.

## Concepts

- Incident
- Caller
- Category
- Impact
- Urgency
- Priority
- Assignment Group
- State
- Resolution Notes

## Example Scenario

A user reports that they cannot access their email account. The service desk creates an incident, assigns it to the correct support group, investigates the cause, and resolves it.

## Steps

1. Review an incident form.
2. Identify the most important fields.
3. Understand how priority can be calculated from impact and urgency.
4. Follow the incident lifecycle:
   - New
   - In Progress
   - On Hold
   - Resolved
   - Closed
5. Document the difference between incident and request.

## Reflection

Incident Management is focused on restoring normal service quickly. Good classification and assignment help reduce resolution time.
